Crystal Collector game

Here's how you play the game

    You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"

    There are four crystals below.

    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow

      • click on the crystal to yeild a random amount of points

      you win by matching your score to the crystals value.

      You lose the game if your total score goes above the crystal value.

      The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

      Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
